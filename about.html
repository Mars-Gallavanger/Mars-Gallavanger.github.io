I'm Chase Burns. In my youth, like any other child, I was always drawing, but the big difference is that I never stopped. Because of this, I ended up graduating as an Illustration major at the Rhode Island School of Design in 2025. I've always been interested in science fiction and old things that nobody else cares about, and I especially like comic books and robot anime (as if you couldn't tell from the stuff on my website).

One of my greatest dreams is to be an outstanding cog in a machine. A lot of people love having absolute creative control, but I personally love helping others and working in a system. I think it's an amazing thing when somebody works on a big group project, like a game or a cartoon, but really puts their all into it. Smaller roles could easily phone it in, but they don't always. That's what I love.
