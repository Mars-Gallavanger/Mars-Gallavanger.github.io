I'm Chase Burns. In my youth, like any other child I was always drawing, but one big difference is that I never stopped. Because of this, I'll graduate from the Rhode Island School of Design in 2025, as an Illustration major. I've always been interested in science fiction and old things that nobody else cares about, and I especially like comic books.

One of my greatest dreams is to be an outstanding cog in a machine. I think it's an amazing thing when somebody works on something mass-produced, like a comic or a cartoon, but really puts their heart and soul into what they're doing on it. They could have easily just phoned it in, but they didn't. I think that's true artistry.
