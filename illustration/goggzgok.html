Fan designs for the Gogg and Z'Gok mobile suits from Mobile Suit Gundam, in the style of Mobile Suit Gundam GQuuuuuuX. These mobile suits appeared very briefly in GQuuuuuuX, but not enough to necessitate drawing up full designs and model sheets, so I wanted to imagine what they'd look like in full.
